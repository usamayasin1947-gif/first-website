About

Haji Aslam Restaurants

Tandoor Restaurant is the Best Restaurant in Karachi – Traditional Cuisine is Pakistan’s most renowned and illustrious Karachi based restaurant. Tandoor is a perfect blend of an amazing fine-dining experience, exceptional customer service, and extraordinarily tempting variety of traditional and contemporary cuisines. Tandoor has become the ultimate dining destination for not only friends and family get-togethers but also for corporate lunches/dinners and moments of the heart. This is the place where memories to be cherished for a lifetime are created. Tandoor is all about turning your ordinary days into special ones and special days into unforgettable ones in a simply outstanding manner. We also embrace innovation to meet our ever-changing customer demands and continue to celebrate our rich culinary legacy while always upholding our health and hygiene standards high that never compromise on quality and authenticity.
